# Chunking Patterns

## What is Chunking?

Breaking information into small, manageable pieces.

## Patterns

### Sentence Chunking

**Bad**: "To complete registration you must first enter your personal information including your full legal name and preferred display name as well as your email address and phone number and then verify your account through the confirmation link sent to your email and then you can proceed to set up your profile."

**Good**: "Register in 3 steps:
1. Your details
2. Verify email
3. Set up profile"

### Section Chunking

Use:
- Clear headings
- Bullet points
- White space
- Numbered steps

### Process Chunking

Break multi-step into:
- 3-5 steps max per screen
- Progress indicators
- Save/resume
- Clear "next" actions
