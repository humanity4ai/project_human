# Examples

## Good: Chunked Process

**Input**: "To buy, add item to cart, review cart, enter shipping, enter payment, confirm order."

**Output**:
"Checkout (3 steps):
1. Shipping
2. Payment
3. Confirm"

## Good: Clear Language

**Input**: "Please utilize the form below to submit your inquiry."

**Output**: "Use this form to contact us."

## Bad: Jargon

**Input**: "Submit your request via the designated portal."

**Output**: "Use the form to submit your request."
