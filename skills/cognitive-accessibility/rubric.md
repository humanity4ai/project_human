# Rubric

## Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | 1 (Poor) | 3 (Acceptable) | 5 (Excellent) |
|-----------|--------|----------|----------------|---------------|
| Simplification | 25% | Complex sentences | Some simplification | Clear, simple |
| Chunking | 25% | No breaks | Some chunks | Well-chunked |
| Clarity | 25% | Jargon throughout | Some jargon | Plain language |
| Structure | 25% | No headings | Some structure | Clear hierarchy |
