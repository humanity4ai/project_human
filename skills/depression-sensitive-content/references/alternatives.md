# Alternative Phrases

## Common Replacements

### Terms to Avoid → Preferred Alternatives

| Avoid | Alternative |
|-------|-------------|
| Suffering from | Living with, experiencing, dealing with |
| Afflicted with | Coping with, managing |
| Victim of | Person who has experienced |
| Mentally ill | Person with mental illness (noun → adjective) |
| Crazy, insane | Use specific terms; avoid |
| Psycho, nut, loony | Avoid entirely |
| Committed suicide | Died by suicide |
| Kill themselves | Take their own life |
| Mental illness | Mental health condition |
| Patient | Person, individual (non-clinical) |
| Disease | Condition, disorder |
| Treatment | Support, help, strategies |

### Phrases to Avoid → Preferred Alternatives

| Avoid | Alternative |
|-------|-------------|
| It's all in your head | That sounds really difficult |
| Just get over it | What helps you feel better? |
| Think positive | What brings you moments of joy? |
| Snap out of it | I'm here for you |
| Others have it worse | Your feelings are valid |
| Get help (vague) | "Reach out to a professional" or specific resources |
| You're not trying | This is hard to manage |
| Be strong | It's okay to feel what you feel |
| Don't be sad | I'm sorry you're feeling this way |

### Sentence Transformations

#### Before: "The depressed user should seek treatment."
**After**: "A person living with depression may benefit from professional support."

#### Before: "Depression victims often experience..."
**After**: "People with depression often experience..."

#### Before: "She committed suicide last year."
**After**: "She died by suicide last year."

#### Before: "The mentally ill population"
**After**: "People with mental health conditions"

#### Before: "Patients who are non-compliant"
**After**: "People who find it hard to follow their treatment plan"

## Context-Specific Alternatives

### Marketing Content
- "Take control of your mental health"
- "Support for your wellbeing"
- "Resources for emotional wellness"

### Support Content  
- "You don't have to face this alone"
- "We're here to help"
- "Support is available"

### Educational Content
- "Understanding depression"
- "What is depression?"
- "Facts about depression"

### Clinical Content (when appropriate)
- "Major depressive disorder"
- "Depressive episode"
- "Treatment-resistant depression"

## Quick Reference Card

| Use This | Not That |
|----------|----------|
| Person living with depression | Depressed person |
| Died by suicide | Committed suicide |
| Mental health condition | Mental illness |
| Person, individual | Patient (non-clinical) |
| Coping with, managing | Suffering from, afflicted |
| Support, help | Treatment (casual) |
| Condition | Disease |
