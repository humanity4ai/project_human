# Clinical Guidelines

## Scope

This skill provides **non-clinical** content guidance. It does not replace professional mental health care.

## What This Skill Does NOT Do

- Diagnose depression
- Provide treatment recommendations
- Offer therapy or counseling
- Replace professional medical advice

## When to Escalate

### Immediate Escalation

Content containing:
- Suicidal ideation ("I want to die," "I can't go on")
- Self-harm references
- Acute crisis language
- Medical emergencies

**Action**: Include crisis resources immediately

### Professional Consultation Needed

Content that:
- Discusses specific treatments
- Makes medical claims
- Provides clinical recommendations
- Describes symptoms as diagnostic criteria

**Action**: Flag for clinical review

## Professional Boundaries

### Appropriate

- Language and tone guidance
- Emotional safety recommendations
- Cognitive accessibility improvements
- Person-first language suggestions
- General mental health awareness

### Inappropriate

- Diagnosis of any condition
- Treatment recommendations
- Medication advice
- Therapy techniques
- Prognosis statements

## Reference Standards

### APA Guidelines

The American Psychological Association provides:
- Person-first language recommendations
- Stigma reduction guidelines
- Research-based terminology

### SAMHSA Guidelines

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration provides:
- Crisis intervention protocols
- Recovery support language
- Cultural competency guidelines

### NICE Guidelines

The National Institute for Health and Care Excellence provides:
- Evidence-based treatment language
- Clinical communication standards

## Cultural Considerations

### Different Cultural Contexts

- Language around mental health varies by culture
- Some cultures prefer identity-first language
- Stigma levels vary across cultures
- Professional help-seeking varies

**Action**: Consider cultural context when applying guidelines

## Documentation

When in doubt, document:
- Why escalation was or wasn't needed
- What resources were provided
- What language was changed and why

This supports accountability and continuous improvement.
