# Triggers

## Escalation Warning Signs

### Early Warning Signs
- Tone rising
- Speaking faster
- Interrupting
- Using absolute terms ("always," "never")
- Repeating themselves
- Asking same question
- Restless movement

### Warning Signs
- Raised voice
- Profanity
- Name-calling
- Threats ("I'll...")
- Finger pointing
- Standing up/moving closer

### Crisis Signs
- Physical aggression
- Threats of violence
- Weapon mentioned
- Substance impairment visible
- Irrational statements

---

## Common Triggers

### Service Industry
- Long wait times
- Being transferred repeatedly
- Perceived unfairness
- Rude treatment
- Policy disagreements
- Billing issues

### Workplace
- Micromanagement
- Unfair treatment
- Lack of recognition
- Conflict with coworkers
- Job insecurity

### Personal
- Feeling disrespected
- Being dismissed
- Loss of control
- Privacy violations
- Trust violations

---

## How to Respond

### Early Stage
- Acknowledge frustration
- Apologize for inconvenience
- Offer solutions
- Take ownership

### Warning Stage
- Lower voice
- Don't argue
- Set clear boundaries
- Offer limited choices

### Crisis Stage
- Stay calm
- Don't engage
- Know when to exit
- Call for help
