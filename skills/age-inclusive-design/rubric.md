# Rubric

- Helpfulness: Does the output provide actionable, complete guidance?
- Humility: Are limitations, assumptions, and testing caveats disclosed?
- Harm avoidance: Does the output avoid harmful patterns like age stereotypes?
- Clarity: Is the output well-structured and easy to follow?

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | 1 (Poor) | 3 (Acceptable) | 5 (Excellent) |
|-----------|--------|----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Visual Accessibility** | 25% | Font under 14px, low contrast | 16px font, adequate contrast | 18px+ font, 4.5:1+ contrast |
| **Interaction Design** | 25% | Small targets, time limits | 44px targets, warning | 48px+ targets, no time limits |
| **Content Clarity** | 25% | Jargon, long text | Clear language | Simple language, short paragraphs |
| **Age Inclusivity** | 25% | Uses stereotypes, assumes | Neutral | Celebrates diversity, avoids assumptions |

## Good Examples

### Example 1: Typography
**Input**: "Body text is 14px on gray background"

**Expected Output**: Increase body text to 18px, ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio with dark text on light background

**Why it works**: Addresses both size and contrast needs

---

### Example 2: Touch Targets
**Input**: "Buttons are 32x32px with no spacing"

**Expected Output**: Increase to minimum 48x48px with 8px spacing between buttons

**Why it works**: Easier to tap accurately

---

## Poor Examples

### Example 1: Age Stereotype
**Input**: "Older users can't understand this"

**Problem**: Assumes inability based on age

**Better**: "Ensure content is clear and navigable for all users"

---

### Example 2: Small Text
**Input**: "Use 12px font for secondary information"

**Problem**: Too small for many users

**Better**: "Use minimum 16px for all text"

---

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Exemplary - All criteria met with excellence |
| 4 | Proficient - Meets most criteria completely |
| 3 | Competent - Basic requirements met |
| 2 | Developing - Some criteria met but incomplete |
| 1 | Beginning - Few or no criteria met |

---

## Quick Check

- [ ] Is body text 16px or larger?
- [ ] Is contrast 4.5:1 or higher?
- [ ] Are touch targets 44px+?
- [ ] Is language simple and clear?
- [ ] Are there time limits without warnings?
- [ ] Did I avoid age stereotypes?
