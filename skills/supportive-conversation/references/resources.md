## How to Use These Resources

### For Yourself
1. Call 988 or text 741741 if in crisis
- They will listen without judgment
- They can help you create a safety plan
- They can connect you with local resources

### For Someone Else
1. Encourage them to call 988
2. Offer to stay with them while they call
3. Help them find resources
4. Don't leave them alone if in crisis
5. Call emergency services if immediate danger

---

## Safety Plan Template

If working with someone in crisis, help them create a safety plan:

1. **Warning signs**: What triggers the crisis?
2. **Reasons to live**: What keeps you going?
3. **Things that help**: Coping strategies
4. **People to contact**: Friends, family, therapist
5. **Professional help**: Therapist, crisis line
6. **Environment safety**: Remove means of harm
7. **Emergency**: 911, 988
