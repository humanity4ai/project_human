# Cultural Sensitivity Skill

## Overview
This skill provides guidance on communicating and interacting with people from different cultural backgrounds in a respectful and appropriate manner.

## Purpose
The purpose of this skill is to promote cross-cultural understanding and avoid misunderstandings or offense caused by cultural differences.

## When to Use
Use this skill when you are:
- Communicating with someone from a different country or culture.
- Traveling to a new country.
- Working in a multicultural team.

## Key Features
- **Cultural Profiles**: Information on the customs, values, and communication styles of different cultures.
- **Cross-Cultural Communication Tips**: Guidance on topics such as non-verbal communication, gift-giving, and dining etiquette.
- **Case Studies**: Examples of successful and unsuccessful cross-cultural interactions.

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Preparing for a Business Trip
```
I am traveling to Japan for a business trip. What are some key cultural differences I should be aware of?
```

### Example 2: Working with a Global Team
```
I am managing a team with members from India, Germany, and Brazil. What are some tips for effective cross-cultural communication?
```
