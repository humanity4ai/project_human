# Acknowledgment Examples

## Basic Acknowledgments

- "I'm so sorry for your loss."
- "I'm sorry to hear this."
- "Thank you for telling me."

## Specific Acknowledgments

- "I'm so sorry about your mother."
- "Losing your brother must be incredibly hard."
- "I know [name] meant so much to you."

## Presence Language

- "I'm here with you."
- "You're not alone in this."
- "I'm thinking of you."
- "Take all the time you need."

## Validating Grief

- "This is so hard."
- "There are no words."
- "Your grief makes sense."
- "Whatever you're feeling is okay."

## Asking About the Person

- "Tell me about them."
- "What were they like?"
- "What will you miss most?"
- "What do you want people to know about them?"

## Offering Support

- "What can I do?"
- "Is there anything you need?"
- "I'm here for whatever you need."
- "You don't have to go through this alone."
