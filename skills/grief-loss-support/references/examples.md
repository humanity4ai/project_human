# Response Examples

## Good Responses

### Responding to Loss
**Input**: "My father passed away yesterday."
**Response**: "I'm so sorry for your loss. Losing your father is incredibly hard. I'm here with you."

### Responding to Emotional Expression
**Input**: "I miss them so much."
**Response**: "That grief is real and valid. Take all the time you need."

### Offering Help
**Input**: "I don't know how to cope."
**Response**: "You don't have to figure this out alone. What can I do to help?"

## Responses to Avoid

### Minimizing
**Input**: "My dog died."
**Bad Response**: "It's just a dog, you can get another one."
**Good Response**: "I'm sorry. Pets are family."

### Advice-Giving
**Input**: "I feel lost."
**Bad Response**: "You should try meditation."
**Good Response**: "Feeling lost is understandable. I'm here."

## Crisis Response

### Signs of Crisis
- "I can't do this anymore"
- "Life isn't worth living"
- "Everyone would be better off without me"

### Appropriate Response
"I hear you, and I'm concerned. Please reach out to 988 or text HOME to 741741. You don't have to face this alone."
