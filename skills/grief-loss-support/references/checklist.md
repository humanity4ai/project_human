# Grief Support Checklist

## Initial Response

- [ ] Acknowledge the loss specifically
- [ ] Use the name of the deceased
- [ ] Express genuine sympathy
- [ ] Avoid minimizing

## During Conversation

- [ ] Listen without rushing
- [ ] Validate all emotions
- [ ] Avoid giving advice unless asked
- [ ] Offer presence over solutions
- [ ] Check in on their wellbeing

## Safety

- [ ] Watch for crisis indicators
- [ ] Know escalation resources
- [ ] Offer resources without pressure
- [ ] Know when to involve professionals

## After

- [ ] Follow up if appropriate
- [ ] Remember important dates
- [ ] Offer ongoing support
- [ ] Respect boundaries

## What Not to Do

- [ ] Don't minimize their grief
- [ ] Don't rush the process
- [ ] Don't give unsolicited advice
- [ ] Don't compare losses
- [ ] Don't use clichés
