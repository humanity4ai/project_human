# Crisis Resources for Grief

## When Grief Becomes Crisis

Grief can sometimes become overwhelming. Include resources when:
- Person expresses desire to die
- Person mentions self-harm
- Grief is preventing basic functioning
- Person shows signs of complicated grief

## US Resources

### 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- Call or Text: 988
- Website: 988lifeline.org
- Available: 24/7

### Crisis Text Line
- Text: HOME to 741741
- Website: crisistextline.org
- Available: 24/7

### SAMHSA National Helpline
- Phone: 1-800-662-4357
- Website: samhsa.gov
- Available: 24/7

## Grief-Specific Support

### The Dougy Center
- Support for children and families
- Website: dougy.org

### Modern Loss
- Resources for processing loss
- Website: modernloss.com

### Bereavement England
- International grief support
- Website: bereavementengland.co.uk

## How to Include Resources

**Do**:
- Offer resources without pressure
- Include multiple contact methods
- Validate before suggesting

**Don't**:
- Don't lecture about getting help
- Don't make them feel broken
- Don't abandon them to find help alone
