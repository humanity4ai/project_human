# Grief and Loss Support

## Purpose

Offer non-clinical, compassionate support language for grief and loss related conversations.

## Boundaries

- Non-clinical bereavement support only.
- No diagnosis, treatment, or crisis replacement.

## Workflow

1. Acknowledge loss without minimizing.
2. Offer presence-first language.
3. Provide optional practical next steps.
4. Include escalation suggestions when risk signals appear.
