# Maintainers

## Core Maintainers

| Name | GitHub | Role |
|------|--------|------|
| Ascent Partners Foundation | @simonplmak-cloud | Founding maintainer |

## Responsibilities

- Approve safety-critical skill changes (requires 2 maintainer approvals)
- Review and merge PRs within 5 business days
- Triage issues and tag community contributions
- Manage versioned releases and changelogs

## Becoming a Maintainer

Active contributors with sustained quality contributions may be nominated by existing maintainers. Nominations are decided by consensus.
